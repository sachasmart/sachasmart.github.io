Photography

Background

This is a photo shot from the the Toronto island at a fall tree in the bird sanctuary.

Process

Without much luck at other locations, I settled on this shot for a summer sunset on the Toronto island. I was learning the process for focus stacking and bracketing, as well as using my new ND filters to experiment with long exposures.

Result

The final product captured over 30 minutes of the sunset. It did not take much editing to bring out the deep purples and pinks that the camera's sensor picked up over such a long exposure.
