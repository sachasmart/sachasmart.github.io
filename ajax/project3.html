The "Policy Wall"

Illustrator and a GSD Attitude

Challenge

Near the beginning of the 2019 federal government elections, our team had an idea to promote the accomplishments of the liberal governments in short, visibly appealing, messages. The "Policy Wall" was the entire side of our warehouse office and became staple for resident's to take photos, Minister Hussen to conduct interviews, and for volunteers to quickly check as they were calling voters.

Process

This project was was a spur of the moment between our media coordinator and myself.

Result

Within 18 hours this idea went from nothing to a completed print and hanging on our campaign office's wall. This was a result of teamwork, optimism, and a GSD attitude.
