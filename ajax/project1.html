Jane Street Bridge and Expansion Project

Background

The Rockcliffe area was chronically impacted by seasonal flooding, and many residents could no longer claim insurance for their properties.

Process

Collaborating with the City of Toronto and Toronto and Region Conservation Authority, an application was submitted to Infrastructure Canada for a $47-million large scale infrastructure project to reduce the impact of flooding. Included in the application were steps that would facilitate a broader flood mitigation system

Result

Historic federal government investment in York South-Weston that will ensure safety for residents, employ local community members, and spur innovative green technology.
